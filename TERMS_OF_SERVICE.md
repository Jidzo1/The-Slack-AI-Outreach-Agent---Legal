## Description of Service

The Slack AI Outreach Agent is a Chrome browser extension that provides:

- Automated scraping of Slack channel member information (names, titles, profile images, user IDs, Slack URLs)
- Export functionality to download scraped member data as CSV or Excel files
- Automated sending of personalized messages through your Slack account
- Message template customization with variable substitution
- Progress tracking for scraping and messaging operations
- Local storage of scraped data and operation state

The Extension operates as a productivity tool that facilitates your own work within your Slack account, automating tasks you would otherwise perform manually.
