# Privacy Policy for The Slack AI Outreach Agent

**Last updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

2MTN AB ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use The Slack AI Outreach Agent Chrome Extension.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Scraped Member Data**: When you scrape Slack channel members, we collect names, titles, profile images, user IDs, team IDs, and Slack URLs from channels you have access to
- **Message Templates**: Message templates you create for personalized messaging
- **Uploaded Data**: CSV or Excel files you upload for bulk messaging operations
- **Operation Preferences**: Your scraping and messaging preferences stored locally

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Scraping Progress**: Information about scraping operations (current progress, status, member count)
- **Messaging Status**: Information about message sending operations (sent, skipped, failed counts)
- **Operation State**: Current state of ongoing operations to enable resuming after browser restarts

### Third-Party Services

- **Slack Platform**: The Extension operates within Slack's platform and sends messages through Slack's infrastructure
- **External Links**: When you click on Slack profile URLs, you may be directed to external Slack pages

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Extract and display Slack channel member information that you have access to
- Send personalized messages through your authenticated Slack account
- Track the progress of scraping and messaging operations
- Enable you to export scraped data as CSV or Excel files
- Resume interrupted operations after browser restarts
- Improve extension functionality and user experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- **Scraped Members**: Your scraped member data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
- **Message Templates**: Your message templates are stored locally
- **Operation State**: Current operation state is stored locally to enable resuming
- **Uploaded Data**: CSV/Excel data you upload is stored locally for messaging operations
- **No Cloud Storage**: We do not store your personal data on external servers
- **No User Accounts**: We do not require or create user accounts

### Data Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- We do not transmit personal information to external servers
- Data stored locally is subject to your device's security settings
- We recommend using appropriate device security measures (passwords, encryption, etc.)

## Third-Party Services

### Slack Platform

- The Extension operates within Slack's platform and requires your authenticated Slack session
- Slack's privacy policy applies to their services
- Messages are sent through Slack's infrastructure using your account
- We are not responsible for Slack's data practices

### External Websites

- Slack profile URLs may direct you to external Slack pages
- We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites
- Please review the privacy policies of external websites

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access Your Data

- All data collected by the Extension is stored locally on your device
- You can access scraped member data through the Extension's interface
- You can view messaging results and operation status in real-time

### Export Your Data

- You can export scraped member data as CSV or Excel files
- You can export messaging results as Excel files
- Exported files are saved to your device's download location

### Delete Your Data

- You can clear all Extension data by uninstalling the Extension
- You can clear Chrome's extension storage data manually
- Data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the Extension

### Control Operations

- You can start, stop, or pause scraping operations at any time
- You can start, stop, or pause messaging operations at any time
- You control when and how the Extension operates

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

### No Data Sharing

We **do not**:

- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with third parties
- Transfer your data to external servers
- Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
- Use your data for analytics or tracking beyond the Extension's functionality

### User Responsibility

You are responsible for:

- All activity that occurs under your account through the Extension
- Ensuring your use complies with Slack's Terms of Service
- Determining appropriate usage frequency and respecting Slack's rate limits
- Verifying the accuracy of data you collect or export
- All message content sent through the Extension

## Children's Privacy
